## 简体中文
- 修复：打开蓝图管理时，名称含空格或连字符的蓝图被误判为「非法名」报错（一次可能上百条误报）。
- 蓝图管理：进入时默认收起所有目录。
- 支持把子分类拖到其它目录，改变其归属。
- 压缩包导入：深层嵌套时弹出分层向导，可自选哪一层作目录、哪一层作子目录，并实时预览，谨慎处理会被丢弃的层。
- 全新分层回收站：删除目录/子目录可进回收站；Windows 文件夹式分层浏览；逐项或多选恢复（恢复目录会连带其下内容）；原目录/子目录已不在时自动归位到「未命名」。删除只有在「应用到存档」时才真正生效（文件删除前已备份）。

## English
- Fix: blueprints whose name contains a space or hyphen were wrongly flagged as an "illegal name" when opening the manager (could be hundreds of false errors at once).
- Manager: all categories start collapsed.
- Drag a subcategory onto another category to re-parent it.
- Zip import: a layered wizard appears for deeply-nested archives — pick which level is the category and which is the subcategory, with a live preview and a clear warning for levels that would be discarded.
- New hierarchical recycle bin: delete categories/subcategories into the bin; Windows-folder-style browsing; restore items individually or by multi-select (restoring a category brings back its contents); orphaned items fall back to "未命名". Deletions only take effect on apply (files are backed up before removal).
